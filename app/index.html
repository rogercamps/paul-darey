Paul Darey

ABOUT

PAUL was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, in 1977 and moved to Barcelona in 1988, where he started his career as a DJ playing at the main house parties in the city. In 2001 he had his first international opportunity in the USA, where he travelled constantly for three years until he moved to Ibiza, where he made his debut together with Roger Sánchez at the Release Yourself pre-party at Café Mambo where he played with all the big headliners that were making Pacha Ibiza pre-parties there, he was dj resident in the world tour for 4 years playing all around the globe. 2004 was the year where he started to play weekly during 3 years at Salvacion Ibiza party in El Divino Ibiza. After that he became resident dj in the most famous club in the world, Space Ibiza, since 2006 till October 2012, he was present in the main events in the mythical club, playing together with international artists such as Richie Hawtin, Carl Cox, Steve Lawler, Nic Fanciulli, Sven Vath, Erick Morillo, Pete Tong, among many others. Paul has shared his music and his tech-house style with Space Ibiza On Tour at the best clubs on the planet like Zouk (Singapore), Tresor (Berlin), Green Valley (Brazil), Ellui (Seoul, South Korea), Queen (Paris), Cube (Rome) Café D’Anvers (Antwerp, Belgium), Qbar (Bangkok, Thailand) XL Beach (Dubai) Cielo (NYC USA) just to name a few. Now he is focus in his own career and decided to jump to a solo dj career.

Summer 2014 was the beginning of Paul’s new projects, he started to play in REVERSE IBIZA party every Wednesday at Privilege Ibiza, the best newcomer underground party of the summer in the Island, and in 2015 also played in Privilege Ibiza and Sankeys several time during the summer and also ran Basswalk party @ Lips Beach Club in Playa d’en Bossa every Thursday playing with dj’s like Cristian Varela, Super Flu, Gabriel Ananda, Marco Bailey, Debborah de Luca and many more… Since December 2014 he is running on Thursday nights in Mataro Barcelona the well known party called The Club where he brought back the electronic music bringing artist like Maceo Plex, Gabriel Ananda, Cristian Varela, Pig&Dan, Rebbot, Andrea Oliva, Tania Vulcano and many more. As a producer, PAUL DAREY is working with prestigious labels such as Deeperfect Records, 303lovers, Stereo Productions, Lapsus Music, Monique Musique, Bush Records, Pornographic Recordings, Cr2 Records, Go Deeva, Sphera Recordings, WTF!, Jekos Music, Night Light Records, Hotfingers, Muzik X-press, Nervous Records, among others. 2012 is the year when Paul gives priority to the studio and the results will come out with new releases and remixes of Stefano Noferini, Gaga and many others. In 2013 he started also a collaboration in the studio with Hannes Bruniic producing and remixing for some of the most prestigious record labels in Underground scene. Paul now has started for 2016 his own production having his first Ep as solo producer signed on Deeperfect Records (Stefano Noreniri’s Label) and also in Noferini’s new Club edition winter compilation. In Jaunary of 2017 his new Track Paul Darey – The Bomb (Deeperfect Records) hitted Beatport top10 during 3 weeks and had the support of all the best djs in the world that had been playing his tracks since now. Once again as the last 3 years Paul has been nominated for the Prestigious Vicious Music Magazine as best dj of the year 2012 and best Tech-House artist for the Vicious Music Awards, as is the past he was nominated 12 times in 5 years for Deejay Magazine in different categories.
